WHAT IS AKPAD?

AKPAD is the most rigorous and demanding decentralized IDO platform on the Binance Smart Chain.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Our team is very professional and experienced in the crypto world .We will carefully select projects, review and verify the origin and legitimacy of the projects. Whether the team’s ideas are innovative enough, and their ability to execute on the project’s vision. Every project launched on AKPAD needs to add 70% of the IDO funds to the liquidity and locked for more than one year. At the same time, all launched projects needs audit to keep AKPAD investors safe and against scams or rug pull.

Tiers levels

AkPad will showcase a fixed tier system based on the number of tokens staked. Project tokens offered on AKPad IDO's will be split into the following levels.

Details of Tiers levels
