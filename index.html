About us

WHAT IS AKPad?

AKPAD is the most rigorous and demanding decentralized IDO platform on the Binance Smart Chain. AKPad will empower crypto currency projects with the ability to distribute tokens and raise liquidity.

WHY CHOOSE US?

AKPad has found a solution to incentivize and reward all token stakers in a way that is inclusive and with a low barrier to entry.

We carefully select projects, review and verify the origin and legitimacy of the project team, the innovation of ideas, and their ability to execute the vision. Every project launched on AKPAD needs to add 70% of the funds raised by IDO to the liquidity pool and lock the pool for more than one year. At the same time, all projects launched will be reviewed and audited to ensure that AKPAD investors are more secure against scams and carpets.
